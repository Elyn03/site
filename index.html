Kazuha Kaedehara

Kaedehara Kazuha (Japanese: 楓かえで原はら万かず葉は Kaedehara Kazuha) is a playable Anemo character in Genshin Impact. A wandering samurai of the once-famed Kaedehara Clan, Kazuha is a temporary crewmember of The Crux.
